Instructions

Guess the PRDLE in six tries.

Each guess must be a five-letter word or name. Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

BE WARNED

The words are randomly chosen from a huge dictionary, and there is a chance you may find a word offensive.
